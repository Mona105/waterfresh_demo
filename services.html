Our Services




Dry Cleaning

WaterFresh is a dry cleaning service that uses specialized solvents in an eco-friendly process. This method is particularly suitable for delicate fabrics that may not withstand traditional washing. We offer a range of services including the cleaning of rugs and carpets, shoes and bags, delicate items, cushions and couches, and formal wear. We also provide a nano coating service for superior protection and longevity of your items. With WaterFresh, you can experience the new age of dry cleaning.


Deep Cleaning

WaterFresh offers comprehensive deep cleaning services that rejuvenate your space, going beyond the surface to target everything from carpets and upholstery to kitchen appliances and hard-to-reach spaces. Our services include Upholstery Renewal, Kitchen Appliances Cleaning, Bathroom Sanitization, Window and Glass Cleaning, Air Duct Cleaning, and Floor Care. We ensure every corner of your home or office is revitalized, resulting in a cleaner, healthier space. Experience the transformation with our meticulous deep cleaning process.


Special Events

WaterFresh offers dedicated cleaning services for special events, ensuring that both you and your venue shine for the occasion. We cater to a variety of events including destination weddings, fashion shows, film shooting events, corporate gatherings, and other special occasions. Our services include ensuring all attire is perfectly pressed and ready. We also provide onsite steam pressing stations for runway-ready outfits at fashion shows, and more. With WaterFresh, you can focus on making beautiful memories while we handle the cleanliness.



Mode Of Services



Pick Up & Drop

We understand your busy schedule. That’s why we offer convenient pick-up and drop-off services for all your cleaning needs. Whether it’s your clothes or cleaning of household items, we ensure our services reach your doorstep. With WaterFresh, experience hassle-free cleaning that comes to you.


Onsite Projects

WaterFresh specializes in cleaning up after construction or renovation projects, leaving your space pristine and ready for occupancy. We also prepare your space for any special event, ensuring it leaves a lasting impression on your guests. For corporates, we ensure a hygienic and comfortable working atmosphere, contributing to productivity and employee well-being.
