Our Services


WaterFresh is an Eco-Friendly Organic Dry Cleaning Company that stands out in the industry for several compelling reasons that set us apart from traditional dry cleaning businesses and even other eco-friendly alternatives.



Events

WaterFresh provides laundry service for events like weddings and corporate events. We create event-specific packages that include a variety of garment cleaning and maintenance services. Planning and executing these large-scale events require accurate attention to detail, and ensuring that attendees look their best is most important. Our specialized event laundry service is designed to relieve the burden of outfit maintenance for event organizers and participants alike. From elegant wedding gowns to finely tailored business suits, we provide a seamless online platform.


Pick & Drop

WaterFresh offers a convenient and efficient pickup and drop service as online has become a crucial part of modern business operations. With the rise of online platforms and mobile apps, customers expect the convenience of scheduling pickups and deliveries at their fingertips. We empower our customers to book, track, and manage their orders with ease. This not only saves time but also builds trust and loyalty, ultimately contributing to the success of our business in today's digitally connected world.

Corporate

WaterFresh laundry services for corporate clients offer a convenient and time-saving solution to one of the daily challenges many businesses face. These services are designed to provide to the specific needs of companies, ensuring that employees always have access to clean and well-maintained uniforms or work attire. By partnering with WaterFresh laundry service, businesses can streamline their laundry operations, reduce the workload on in-house staff, and maintain a professional appearance. WaterFresh laundry services often provide eco-friendly options, promoting sustainability and social responsibility within the corporate world.


Retail Services

WaterFresh laundry services for households have gained immense popularity in recent years, offering a convenient and time-saving solution for busy individuals and families. Our service provides a hassle-free way to manage the never-ending task of washing, drying, and folding clothes. With just a few clicks on a mobile app or website, customers can schedule pick-up and delivery, select their preferred settings, and customize special instructions. The laundry will be collected from your doorstep, professionally cleaned, and returned to you, all within a specified timeframe. This not only frees up your valuable time but also ensures that your clothes are cleaned and maintained to a high standard. We offer eco-friendly options and often have a user-friendly platform that makes the entire process seamless and efficient.
